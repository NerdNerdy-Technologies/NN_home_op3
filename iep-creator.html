IEP Creator for Parents

Individualised approach according to each child's need.

A major hurdle that parents of special needs face is the lack of direction when it comes to intervention. Parents are not equipped with a well structured plan to monitor in their child’s skill development.

NerdNerdy has developed an easy to use application, where parents of children with neurodevelopmental disorders like Autism, ADHD, Cerebral Palsy and more, can fill in a skill evaluation test and automatically generate a professional IEP for their child.
