Special Needs Management Application
NerdNerdy has created a first-of-its-kind technology tool to aid professionals working with children with special needs.

Using this application, professionals can perform Screening, conduct Assessment and diagnose 12 neurodevelopmental disorders including Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Social communication Disorder, Sensory Processing and more.

It further empowers therapists to create the child’s Individualised Educational Plan (IEP), track the progress and generate standard reports.
