Welcome to NerdNerdy.

A Research-driven, technology and learning resource company in the field of Special Needs and Education.

A huge gap in the health care system for special needs children and the education sector vis-à-vis actual learning, led to the genesis of Nerdnerdy.

NerdNerdy's vision is to improve quality intervention in Special Need & bringing better learning outcomes in the classrooms.

Assistive Technology for children with Special Needs

Technology can level the playing field for children with Special Needs and can open wide variety of oppurtunities for them. Nerdnerdy strives to bring in best Assistive Technology solution to empower Parents, Rehabilitation Professionals, Schools and Institutions, to enhance Quality Intervention.


1. Spl.EdTech for Rehab Professionals

Our cutting-edge technology tools provide an end to end solution, from screening to assessment, development of individualised education plan (IEP), customized intervention resources, online training modules, progress tracking and standard report generation.

Register Here
2. IEP creator for Parents of Special Needs ( Individualized Education Plan)

A major hurdle that parents of special needs face is the lack of direction when it comes to intervention. Parents are not equipped with a well structured plan to monitor in their child’s skill development.

NerdNerdy has developed an easy to use application, where parents of children with neurodevelopmental disorders like Autism, ADHD, Cerebral Palsy and more, can fill in a skill evaluation test and automatically generate a professional IEP for their child.

Demo Link
3. Single Screening Tool for Peadiatricians and Healthcare Professionals for Neurodevelopmental Disorders

Peadiatricians and other Healthcare Professional are the first point of contact for any parent, therefore it is imperative that they are equipped to screen children who may be at risk of Neurodevelopmental disorder.

Currently, there is no tool to screen all the Neurodevelopmental disorder in one screening scale thus, each disorder has to be screen seperatly using specific screening scale for a perticular disorder. NerdNerdy's unique technology tool has tired to address this issue by developing single screening scale for screening all the 11 Neurodevelopmental disorder.
