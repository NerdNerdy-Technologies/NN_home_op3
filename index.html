IEP ( Individualized Education Plan) creator for Parents

A major hurdle that parents of special needs face is the lack of direction when it comes to intervention. Parents arent equipped with a well structured plan to progress in their child’s skill development. Thus NerdNerdy created an easy to use application, where parents of children with neurodevelopmental disorders like Autism, ADHD, Cerebral Palsy and more, can fill out a skill evaluation test and automatically generate a professional IEP for their child.

Neurodevelopmental disorders Screening tool for Peadiatricians and healthcare professionals

A Screening software specially designed for Paediatrician and other front line healthcare professionals. Paediatricians and health care professionals are the first point of contact for any parent, thus it is imperative that these professionals are equipped to screen children who may be at risk of Neurodevelopmental disorder. Our technology tool helps the front line professionals to screen all the Neurodevelopmental disorders through a single screening tool. The data is being collected & analysed to make it a standardized tool in near future
