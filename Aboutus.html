About Us

For long the status quo has dictated the way we live, learn and teach. And though it may have served the interests of the society till a certain period, we never settled with it.

We are a team of professionals whose endeavour is to find better and creative solutions to the issues faced by our society in Special Needs Healthcare and Education.

Using state-of-the-art technology that is backed by research, we hope to give wings to our dream of providing a better life for the young beautiful minds, including those who are special or gifted.

We are a team of experienced professionals - Child Psychologists, Rehabilitation Professionals, Speech therapists, Occupational therapists, Health Care Experts and Educationists.

Founder & CEO

Our team is led by our Founder & CEO Dr. Mukta Vasal.

Dr. Vasal is a Senior Consultant Child and Adolescent Psychologist with over 25 years of experience in Healthcare (special needs) and Education.

She has earned honours in child development, a bachelor’s degree in education, a master’s degree in psychology. She has done her doctorate in human development and family studies from MS University of Baroda.

She has undertaken certifications in super-specialised areas such as Gifted Education (Purdue University), The TEACCH method for autistic children (University of North Carolina), Sensory Integration and Praxis Test (SIPT) administration & measurement, (University of Southern California).

Co-Founder

Chahat Vasal

Chahat has been working with special needs children since a young age and has good understanding of the neurodevelopmental disorders and the assistive technology required to address the gaps in this eco-system.

She has developed ‘Multiple Intelligence kit’ (Copyrighted) to identify the strengths of children with Autism. Her research paper on this subject has been published in a reputed journal. She has also developed JumpHats, an online application for creating e-teaching-learning tools, and is responsible for creating & developing new educational resources. She is currently pursuing her engineering in Mechatronics (Robotics and Automation) from Manipal Institute of Technology.

Our Advisors

Dr. Prerna Mohite, Ph.D.

Professor Emeritus, Department of Human Development and Family Studies, M S University of Baroda

Prof Mohite has over three decades of experience in Child/Human development with specialization in Early Child Development and Education. She has worked in key dimensions of early education - curriculum for infants and pre-schoolers, assessment, pedagogy, training Early Childhood Education and Care (ECCE) personnel, learning disability, development of learning materials for children, training manuals for teachers training, development of tools and tests of assessment and other related areas.

 

She has advised / been associated with International and Indian organisations of repute - NCERT, Indian Council of Social Sciences, Indian council of Agriculture, WHO, UNICEF.

Further, she has been a consultant to Governments of Yemen, Philippines, Maldives, Thailand and Malaysia on various aspects of ECCE.

A well-known leader in the health care (Diagnostics) sector with over three decades of experience.  He led the transformational journey of one of the most successful companies in India – Dr. Lal Path Labs.

He is a regular speaker at various national and international forums and business schools. He has earlier been associated with Ranbaxy, Monsanto and Hindustan Lever. Mr. Manchanda is an alumnus of IIMA.
